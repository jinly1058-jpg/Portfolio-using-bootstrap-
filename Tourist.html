Tourist Management System

Online Tourist Booking and Travel Management Platform

Project Overview

The Tourist Management System is designed to simplify travel planning and booking. Users can search destinations, book packages, view hotel details, and manage their trips through a single platform.

Features

  • User Registration & Login
  • Tour Package Booking
  • Hotel Reservation
  • Destination Search
  • Admin Management Panel
  • Online Payment Integration

Technologies Used
